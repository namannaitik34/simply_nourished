Why the 100-Day Nourish Plan exists

The 100-Day Nourish Plan isn't a quick fix or a restrictive challenge — it's a scaffold for reconnecting with your natural rhythm. Life gets busy and it's easy to default to convenience foods or skip restorative practices. This plan helps you build a gentle routine that fits your life: nourishing breakfasts, short walks, regular sleep, and brief moments of reflection. By emphasizing consistency and compassion, the plan helps small actions become automatic, and automatic actions become lasting change.

Mindful living — small practices

Nourishment includes both external choices and internal habits. Try these small practices to support the plan:

  • Breathe for one minute before each meal to settle digestion.
  • Fill half your plate with vegetables or fruit at one meal per day.
  • Choose whole foods as a first option and notice how you feel after meals.
  • Use a simple weekly tracker to note progress rather than perfection.
